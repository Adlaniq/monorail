KL Monorail

The KL Monorail is an intra city public transit system that links many key destinations within Kuala Lumpur.

Operated as part of the RapidKL system by Rapid Rail, a subsidiary of Prasarana Malaysia, it is one of the components of the Klang Valley Integrated Transit System.
